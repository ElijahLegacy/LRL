Land

Date: 15.03.2010  |  Posted by Elijah Dela Cruz  |  Filed under London Residency Limited

Located in the Town of Cleveland in West Rowan County, the Brown site offers 104 acres and is located 10 miles from Interstate 77, 12.5 miles from Interstate 40 and 15 miles from Interstate 85, with frontage along US 70. All major utilities are available.

Cleveland is known for it’s picturesque landscapes and charming lifestyle. Cleveland is strategically located within close proximity to two cities and two of the largest municipalities in North Carolina’s Piedmont region. 3.5 million people live within a 50-mile radius of Cleveland.

The community offers access to the amenities of the city: restaurants, shops, and theaters, with the charm of small-town living, abundant acreage for equestrian and cattle ranches, and active outdoor actives such as hunting or fishing, hiking or biking.

The Brown site is located across from a Daimler Truck North America facility where brands such as Freightliner and Western Star Class-8 trucks are manufactured. It offers an ideal location for automotive suppliers and other related businesses. A recent participant in the Duke Energy Site Readiness Program, the Brown Site is aggressively priced and additional acreage is available.
