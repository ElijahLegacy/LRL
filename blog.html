Customer Review

Posted by Sebastian Larenza  •  Filed under Kadokawa Corporation

Where do I start? Oh yes, Rick & Shpresa are beyond incredible human beings. The day I meet them, I was walking by and the left corner of my eye caught a stunning necklace made with crystals. I turned to look at it & realized such amazing decor on their windows. I mean beautiful crystals. My first impression was, wow a crystal place, let me go in and check them out so, I entered the business and who was there to greet me the one & only Shpresa. Who was peeking in from their desk in the back the one & only Rick. Shpresa so kind asked, how may I help you? Now at this point I saw several desks so I was thrown off, thinking it was a crystal place but I asked her anyway, do you sell crystals? Her response in the most humbling way, we manage over 200 properties. My jaw dropped because I felt like I embarrassed myself, but she quickly made me feel right at home. My god, their office was the most beautiful office I've ever witness in my life! From the art work to all the details, just spectacular. What made my experience even greater is Rick & Shpresa from the moment I've meet them, I literally stop by maybe once or twice a week just to hang out for a few minutes and let me tell you this they receive me with the same energy every single time, with open arms, open heart, open mind and I'm beyond grateful. That's the type of energy they bring to the table. Such beautiful & miraculous people. You can check for yourself their achievements speak for themselves but, it's bigger then that. Their character speaks more volume then any accolades. I clearly see why & how they manage so many properties. I know for sure when the time comes & I own a few more properties, it'll be London Residency Limited (LRL) managing it. (FYI, there location in Bronxville, NY) PS - 5 Star Service all around.
